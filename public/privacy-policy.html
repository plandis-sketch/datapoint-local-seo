Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 26, 2026

Data Point Marketing ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website datapoint.marketing and associated applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our services, including our Meta (Facebook) integrated application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our application, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Meta Account Data: When you authorize our application, we receive an access token that allows us to access your Meta Ads account data. This includes ad campaign information, ad performance metrics, account identifiers, and related advertising data.
  • Authentication Information: We collect the access tokens generated through Meta's OAuth authorization flow to maintain your authenticated session.
  • Usage Data: Basic information about how you interact with our application, such as access times and features used.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To retrieve and display your Meta Ads account data for reporting and analytics purposes.
  • To generate and manage access tokens that enable the connection between our application and your Meta Ads account.
  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We may share information when you have given us explicit permission to do so.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our application, solely for the purpose of performing services on our behalf.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal processes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your Meta Ads data and access tokens only for as long as necessary to provide our services to you. When you disconnect your Meta account or request deletion, we will delete your access tokens and any cached data within 30 days.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Access tokens are stored securely and transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS).

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Upon request, we will delete all stored access tokens and associated Meta Ads data within 30 days.
  • Revoke Access: You can revoke our application's access to your Meta account at any time through your Facebook Business Integrations settings.

7. Meta Platform Data Use

Our use of information received from Meta APIs adheres to the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies, including:

  • We only request permissions necessary for our application's functionality.
  • We do not use Meta data for purposes unrelated to our application's stated functionality.
  • We do not transfer or sell Meta data to data brokers, information resellers, or any other third parties.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date above.
